I'm an undergrad at Harvard studying math.

I have been to school with sons of Guatemalan immigrants and Emirati oyster farmers, with the daughters of Indian cobblers and British royalty. I grew up moving from Kentucky to New York to Ohio, then to Delhi, Abu Dhabi, London, San Francisco, and now, Boston.

I work on the science of deep learning, and am broadly fascinated by scaling and universality. I'm applying to CS PhD programs this fall. Outside of my research I'm interested in the Indian industrialization story and the role that AI will play in its next chapter.
