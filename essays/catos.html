ArchLinux and the Duomo

- short history of both linux and the duomo In general, operating systems are examples of magic, manifest in the flesh. They are well and truly the software that brings bare metal to life. Before an operating system, we just have a mass of silicon and chips that respond in curious ways when you poke them with electricity. After an operating system, we have a machine that implements abstract computation on an electronic substrate, and can do things useful to human beings! sims bw OSs and ancient architecture: - arch because it's resource constrained/has skin in the game (ie. roofs can fall) - eng meets artistry -- systems design component is involved - megaproject in the sense of millions of man hours and huge teams diffs - duomo pushed sota - duomo is primarily artistic/symbolic ? diff bw this essay and pg's "hackers and painters"
