People

I think it's important to have idols. Let's be clear--not to idolize people, but to have a set of people who you deeply admire for one reason or the other; real or fictional. I've found that having a set of people I respect and aspire to be like helps me make the right decisions on a day-to-day basis--if I don't think my idols would make a certain decision, neither do I.

You can't work towards your ideal future reality if you don't even know what the reality looks like--having concrete examples of role models who you want to mould certain aspects of your life around helps with that. I'm generally sceptical of "inspirational quotes" but I've also included quotes, by those who I look up to, and otherwise, that have resonated over the years or reconsider my position on something.
